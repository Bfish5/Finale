About the Shop

Our Story

Neighborhood Barber is a locally owned shop built on sharp technique, friendly conversation, and attention to the details that make a cut feel finished. From classic tapers to modern skin fades, we keep your look clean and consistent.

Every appointment is unrushed and tailored—so you leave feeling confident, lined-up, and ready for whatever’s next.
